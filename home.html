The process simplified

Define

I will connect with you and discuss your company goals, wishes, and legal issues you are facing that inhibit you from reaching those goals.

Strategize

I work with you to decipher your company preferences. With this understanding I will then develop a tailored strategy for your companies success.

Draft

I will write and thoroughly edit an agreement that suits your goals and resolves any issues. Once that is complete I will then answer all of your questions regarding the agreement.

Negotiate

I will conduct negotiations with the other party and collaborate with you to find creative solitions that fit your goals and needs.

Finalize

Once negotiations are finished, I will finalize your agreement and execute it. This ensures no time is wasted and you can focus on reaching your company goals.
